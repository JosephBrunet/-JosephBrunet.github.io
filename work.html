Current research

My current research is focused on vascular biomechanics. I work at identifying and quantifying the mechanisms behind vascular failure and especially aortic dissection. To this end, I performed in situ experiments combining mechanical testing and imaging techniques. This approach makes it possible to investigate the evolution and changes of aortic microstructure during rupture.
